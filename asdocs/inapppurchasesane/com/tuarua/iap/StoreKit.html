verifySubscription()method 
public function verifySubscription(productId:String, receipt:Receipt, type:int):VerifySubscriptionResult

Verify the validity of a subscription (auto-renewable, free or non-renewing) in a receipt

This method extracts all transactions matching the given productId and sorts them by date in descending order. It then compares the first transaction expiry date against the receipt date to determine its validity.

Parameters

productId:String — The product id of the subscription to verify.
 
receipt:Receipt — The receipt to use for looking up the subscription.
 
type:int (default = NaN) — SubscriptionType.autoRenewable or SubscriptionType.nonRenewing.

Returns
VerifySubscriptionResult —
